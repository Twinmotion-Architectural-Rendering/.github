## 🧠 Overview

**Twinmotion** is a real-time visualization application powered by Unreal Engine that transforms CAD, BIM, and 3D models into photorealistic images, cinematic animations, interactive presentations, and immersive virtual experiences with minimal setup. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Its intuitive workflow enables designers to import models directly from popular architectural and engineering software, enhance scenes with realistic materials, vegetation, weather effects, lighting, and animated assets, then generate high-quality visual content in real time. The platform is widely used throughout architecture, construction, landscape design, product visualization, and urban planning. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Designed for architects, visualization artists, interior designers, landscape architects, engineers, developers, and Windows users.
